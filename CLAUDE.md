## Backlog User Ideas

When the user asks to "save an idea/feature to backlog" (or save for later), append it to `~/backlog.md` in free format. Distill all relevant context around the idea or a feature, so that in future it could be easily implemented after design decisions made. Before writing it down, elaborate on the idea asking questions to learn the details. After idea concept is completed - append it into the file.
